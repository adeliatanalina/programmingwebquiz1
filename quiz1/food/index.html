Local Food

Some iconic dishes from Surabaya.

Lontong Balap

Rice cake with bean sprouts, fried tofu, lentho, and savory soy broth.

Rawon

Beef soup with a dark, aromatic broth from keluak, great with sprouts and salted egg.

Rujak Cingur

Mixed salad with lontong, vegetables, and petis shrimp-paste dressing.

Tahu Tek

Fried tofu and egg with lontong, beansprouts, and sweet-savory peanut sauce.
