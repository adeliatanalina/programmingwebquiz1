Tourist Places

Some must-visit attractions in Surabaya.

Alun-Alun

Located in Central Surabaya, great for evening walks and photos.

KBS (Zoo)

Historic city zoo with diverse fauna and a family-friendly place.

Mangrove

Boardwalks through coastal mangroves, a very calm and scenic place.

Tunjungan

Iconic street with heritage buildings, cafés, and lights.
